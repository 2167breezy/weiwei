Editor's Letter 编辑手记

点击展开信件 Click to unfold

很多人因为一项技能定义自己，而我，总是因为一个个问题。

什么样的空间，会让人愿意停下来？

什么样的内容，会让陌生人开始聊天？

什么样的产品，会让人愿意一次次回来？

什么样的机制，会让一次相遇变成长久的关系？

这些问题，让我学习空间设计、研究社区，也开始探索 AI。

因为我始终相信：环境塑造关系，社区孕育连接，技术应该放大人的创造力，而不是取代人与人。

我真正感兴趣的是「人与空间」「人与人的连接」、「人与自己」在未来，会留下什么。

让一个地方，值得停留；也因为人与人的连接，值得一次次回来。

Most people define themselves by a skill. I define myself by questions.

What kind of space makes people want to pause?

What kind of content makes strangers start a conversation?

What kind of product makes people want to keep coming back?

What kind of mechanism turns an encounter into a lasting relationship?

These questions led me to study spatial design, communities, and now AI.

Because I've always believed: environments shape relationships, community breeds connection, and technology should amplify human creativity — not replace it.

What I'm truly interested in is what remains — between people and space, between people and people, between people and themselves — in the future.

A place worth staying. And because of the connections between people — worth coming back to, again and again.

—— 魏婧亭 / Wei Jingting
